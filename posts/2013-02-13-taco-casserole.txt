title: Taco Casserole
---

my mom has made this since i was little. i love it. brett loves it. hopefully caylin will love it :)

preheat oven to 350

brown up 1 lb of ground beef in a pan  
throw in a can of light red kidney beans drained  
add 1 packet of taco seasoning  
mix and let cook on low heat for a while

crush up some tortilla chips in the bottom of a baking pan

pour in beef/bean mixture

spread 8oz of sour cream on top, followed by shredded cheddar cheese and diced tomatoes/whatever else you want to toss on

bake for about 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly

serve with chips and a cold beer! i almost like it better reheated the next day.
