title: Freshly Baked USB Drives

[![](http://www.vavolo.com/C/FD-FB-3E_0.JPG)](http://www.vavolo.com/Func_info.asp,Func_ID,66,Func_Name,Freshly+Baked+USB+Drives,,.htm) i was at work the other day and my flash drive finally bit the dust. i remembered seeing ones online shaped like sushi so i got curious and started googling for USB drives shaped like other stuff. i came across [these little suckers](http://www.vavolo.com/Func_info.asp,Func_ID,66,Func_Name,Freshly+Baked+USB+Drives,,.htm) and i think i would buy one of every kind if i could afford it. they’re so cute but so overpriced.

i ended up going to buy.com and buying a 4GB flash drive for $14.99, which is a damn good deal. but… i deserve a pat on the back for resisting the urge. :wink: when i become a millionaire, i’m buying one shaped like pizza. maybe a donut, too. although by that time, they probably won’t exist anymore and we’ll all be planting hard drives in our foreheads.
