copenhagen is beautiful. nothing like i’d ever expected, this is incredible. we’ve been here 2 and a half days and so far we’ve visited the copenhagen business college, parliament, the royal palace, an art museum (monet & pissarro, yesss), the denmark design company, tivoli gardens amusement park, went on a canal tour, 6 bars, made my first alcohol purchase ever, sang karaoke with a bunch of danish college drunk crazies (frank sinatra and dolly parton are apparently big karaoke hits here!!), and i could go on forever. we’ve been busy, we walk EVERYWHERE, beautiful weather, lovely people, awesome atmosphere and so much to see and do and i still can’t believe i’m here. i’m not going to want to go home.
