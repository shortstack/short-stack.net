![shortcake](/content/images/2010/09/shortcake.gif "shortcake")a few nights ago, i had a dream that brett and i had a baby.

a few mornings ago, i spent the morning nauseated and puking.

that was weird enough for me.

however.

i was just informed by my supervisor that, a few nights ago, the same night, she also had a dream about me. a dream about me wherein brett and i had bought a house, i was pregnant, and she was helping me deck out the baby room in strawberry shortcake decor. the cartoon, not the food. also strange because i *love* strawberry shortcake (ever since i was little), and she does not know that.

i’m this close to driving to the rite-aid down the street and buying a home pregnancy test.
