title: Crispy Ranch Chicken Tenders
---

these are also one of my favorites, and i think they taste better than frying them.

thaw out 8-10 chicken breast tenderloins

preheat oven to 350 degrees

coat tenderloins in ranch dressing  
toss in a mixture of bread crumbs (i use the storebought stuff) and crushed corn flakes

arrange in baking dish and bake for 30-40 minutes! that’s it!
