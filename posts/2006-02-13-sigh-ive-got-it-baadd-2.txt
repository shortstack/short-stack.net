title: Sigh, I've Got it BAADD

i almost forgot the best part of friday night. eric clapton’s “wonderful tonight” started playing while we were all dancing… so adam picked me up with my arms around his neck so my feet were about 2 feet off the ground and i was right up there with him and he did it for the whole song. i know it made it quite uncomfortable holding me there like that but (shh, don’t tell adam) i almost cried. granted, i had had a few drinks, but it made me so happy. little things like that mean more than anything. and so many people kept looking at me with my dorky huge smile going “awww” and it made me realize once again one of the gazillion reasons i’m in love with this kid.
