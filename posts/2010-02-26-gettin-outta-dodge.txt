we are leaving this afternoon. we are leaving and driving to mooresville, and i *never* thought i would be this excited about just going to mooresville, but i am. we’re getting out of our little apartment, and we’re taking loki with us, and we are going to stay at brett’s parents’ house for the weekend.

for those unfamiliar with the carolinas–[mooresville is Race City USA](http://www.racecityusa.org/)! hot dayum! it’s located about 25 minutes outside of charlotte, and is home to lake norman and dale earnhardt. and also my grandparents.

here is my to-do list. i don’t know what his consists of but i assume it will be similar.

* go to biscuitville. apparently, south carolina does not believe in biscuitville. i need my fix–their chicken biscuits are to die for.
* go to what-a-burger. i’m addicted to cheeseburgers, and brett is addicted to cherry lemon sundrop (which you also cannot find in south carolina).
* sleep in–it’s what i do best.
* wear sweatpants for approximately 90% of the weekend.
* enjoy the sunshine.

i have such high hopes!

enjoy your weekend, everyone. :)
