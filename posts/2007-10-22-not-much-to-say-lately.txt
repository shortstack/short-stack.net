i had a great week/weekend. i think i partied a bit more than i should have but the only thing bad to come from that was going to my business writing class a wee bit hungover. i seem to have misplaced a 5th of whiskey in this apartment building and i’m convinced that i want an english bulldog. ASU lost to georgia southern, i got to hang out with a lot of old friends, loki ate 2 months’ worth of my birth control pills (again), and i got to talk to adam on the phone saturday morning.

my mom and her boyfriend came up to boone this afternoon and had lunch with me. we wandered around wal-mart for a good bit, lounged around the apartment, and laura and i had a quiet night at home. we gorged on pizza and watched black sheep (highly recommend it, scary movie–not chris farley movie) and the wedding date. for the first time in forever, i think i might go to sleep at a reasonable hour.

it has been a glorious past week and i’m pretty sure it’s only going to get ~~better~~ more chaotic. this friday i’m going to the hurricanes game in raleigh and driving home that night for the LAN saturday morning. then i assume we will be partying pretty hard that night, halloween that week, and then THE LOVE OF MY LIFE IS FLYING HOME AND I WILL BE THE HAPPIEST GIRL IN THE WORLD. i don’t think i will be able to breathe right or stop smiling or giggling for the next couple of *months*.

that’s what’s been going on. i just need to magically get rid of my allergies and everything will be just lovely… ha.
