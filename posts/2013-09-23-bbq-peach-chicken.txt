title: BBQ Peach Chicken

[![20130923_183329](/content/images/2013/09/20130923_183329-1024x768.jpg)](/content/images/2013/09/20130923_183329.jpg)

chicken breasts or thighs  
1 peach, peeled/sliced  
1 jar peach preserves  
1/4 cup worcestershire sauce  
1/2 to 1 cup bbq sauce (i estimated)  
1/2 onion, diced, or 1 tsp onion powder  
1 tsp minced garlic  
olive oil

heat olive oil in pan, add garlic and onions, simmer  
add chicken, brown on both sides  
add peach slices, preserves, worcestershire sauce, bbq sauce — mix  
reduce heat to medium low/simmer  
simmer for an hour/hour and a half, stirring occasionally

this was pretty tasty with creamy mashed taters–sweet & salty worked out nicely. :)

i took [the pioneer woman’s recipe](http://thepioneerwoman.com/cooking/2011/08/peach-whiskey-barbecue-chicken/) and tweaked it. we were out of whiskey (*gasp*) and i added whole peach slices. also subbed onion powder for onions, since i don’t like them.
