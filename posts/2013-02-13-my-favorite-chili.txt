title: My Favorite Chili
---

cook up some rotini pasta and set aside.

brown 1 lb of ground beef in a pan  
add 1 can of light red kidney beans, drained

in a pot (this could also be done in a slow cooker), pour in 2 cans of tomato soup and 1 cup of water  
add chili powder, cayenne, red pepper, and black pepper to taste  
pour in beef and bean mixture

cook over low/med heat for a little while (this is totally up to you)

pour over rotini pasta and throw some cheese on top (i love to add a slice of american cheese) :)
