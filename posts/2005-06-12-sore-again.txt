my grandparents and uncle and cousin have been in town the past 2 days because my mom’s birthday was friday so we all went out and had a family dinner last night. i got the balls to leave a napkin with my name and number on the table before we left because our waiter was definitely gorgeous and i had to do it. i’ve never done that before so i was proud of myself. i was even more proud when i got a txt message and a call back, although the call was at 1AM and i was busy dancing and didn’t have my phone. cool points for me!

i went to the club last night again with ryan, chris, and kayla (sp?) and we had a blast. a few other guys they knew were there and i bumped into some old friends so it turned out to be yet another great night. :smile: this time i didn’t leave the dance floor *at all*, not even to go to the bathroom, for 3 hours. i was having so much fun, and ryan and chris kept kayla and i dancin’ all night long. i about died when they started playing a remix of a journey song–SO COOL!

we left the club at 3 and drove back to ryan’s. i fell asleep in the car on the way back so i had a nice little nap to keep me going while i drove from ryan’s to my place. it’s a good 15-20 minute drive so i needed that power nap.

i woke up at 1:30 this afternoon and it felt so good to sleep in. it’s going to make me wish i could do it again tomorrow but… nooo. gotta work. gay. oh well. i’m gonna shower and find something to get into! :cool:
