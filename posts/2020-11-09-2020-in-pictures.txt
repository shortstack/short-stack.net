title: A Year In Pictures
---

i started out down this rabbithole thinking i'd be able to pick a couple pictures per month. and i was so wrong.

i quickly realized that there are more amazing memories with my girls than i can fit in a single post.

i am so thankful for these 3--going through the last 11 months of pictures, especially during a year like 2020, took over an hour. and that was scrolling quickly.

i can't imagine life without these little faces. i tell them every day that i love them to pieces, but i hope they forever know how much they mean to me.
