i haven’t heard back from the doctor about her biopsy, so i don’t have much news to share other than loki is doing better every day. she has been playing with her toys today and got excited about steak, so she can’t be in too much pain at this point. :)

brett and i stayed home today. he watched his football and played battlefield. i cleaned the house and worked on websites. actually, i *tried* to work on websites, but about every 5 minutes loki would come up and ram me in the arm with her cone and/or punch me in the leg. and then she’d whine. and i’d either take her outside or pet her for a while. i can’t tell if something is genuinely wrong or if she’s just milking it now.
