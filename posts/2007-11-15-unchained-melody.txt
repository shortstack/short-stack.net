it’s snowing outside, i’m drinking hot cocoa all bundled up in cozy blankets, planted in front of the TV watching [ghost](http://imdb.com/title/tt0099653/). unchained melody is playing and i don’t think i could ever get tired of this song. it’s been one of my favorite songs since i was a little girl and if there were ever a song that made me wish i grew up in a different time period, that would be it.

if that song doesn’t play at my wedding, someone will lose their head. end of story.
