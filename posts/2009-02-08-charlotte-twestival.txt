i was reading [iJustine](http://ijustine.com)‘s blog and watched her video promoting the [Twestival](http://twestival.com) on **february 12, 2009**. she’s promoting the one in LA, but there are [Twestivals in cities worldwide](http://twestival.com).

[Twestival](http://twestival.com) is what it sounds like– [twitter](http://twitter.com) + festival = [Twestival](http://twitter.com/twestival)!!!1

> **WHEN:** February 12, Doors open at 6 pm, Festivities begin at 7
>
> **WHERE:** Cans Bar and Canteen, Uptown Charlotte,  
> 500 W. 5th Street, 28202
>
> **WHAT:** Charity event for CHARITY:WATER.  
> Network with Charlotte Leaders in social media, technology, & business, listen to great music, and play fun games for prizes (including a cruise!).
>
> **COST:** Tiered pricing – Give what you can for a ticket.  
> Limited number of $5.00 tickets available.  
> $100 VIP tickets come with plenty of perks.

if i can, [i’d love to go to the one in charlotte](http://www.amiando.com/twestivalcharlotte.html). it all depends on my money situation. that and i have class until 2 on thursdays so i’m gonna try and see what i can do.

SO, if i can’t go, i’m posting about it here in the hopes that someone somewhere *will* go. you can help out by [donating money to charity:water](http://www.charitywater.org/twestival/), [entering their t-shirt design competition](http://desiredhearts.com/twestival/), [uploading/buying music from twestival.fm](http://www.twestival.fm/), or by attending a Twestival near you. or all 4. :)

> Unsafe water and lack of basic sanitation cause 80% of all sickness and disease, and kill more people every year than all forms of violence, including war.
>
> Many communities in developing nations often have a plentiful supply of clean drinking water just below the ground, but no way to get to it.
>
> This is where charity: water and their partner organizations come in. Drilling a well can cost from $4,000 – $12,000 USD and many living on less than $1 a day cannot afford one in their community, even if the money is combined.
>
> Twestival cities might consider setting a goal to raise enough money from their event to fund a well project in a developing country, starting at $4,000 USD.
