i was sick of staring at my old layout, so i “hired” [a friend](http://soeasilydistracted.com) [of mine](http://soeasilydistracted.com), [amanda](http://soeasilydistracted.com), to whip up this badass drawing for a new design.

i told her i’d pay her, and she said NO WAY DON’T GIVE ME MONEY, but because of the sheer awesomeness of what she created (i’m a freaking cartoon, dude!), i couldn’t *not* show her my appreciation.

so i bought her a present on etsy (she doesn’t know what it is yet–it’s a secret) and i’ve decided that the next time she comes to our apartment, and she tells me that, no, she does not want anymore shots of whiskey, i will not whine. unless it’s a little one.

THANK YOU, AMANDA. YOU ROCK.
