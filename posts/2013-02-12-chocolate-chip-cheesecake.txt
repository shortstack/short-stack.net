title: Chocolate Chip Cheesecake

![chocolate chip cheesecake](/content/images/2013/02/cheesecake.jpg)

3 8oz blocks cream cheese  
3/4 cup sugar  
1 bag mini chocolate chips (this makes it super rich, so  
1 tsp vanilla extract  
1 egg (or 2-3 if you want it super fluffy)

blend until fluffy.

for the crust…

1 package of mashed up graham crackers (i generally crunch them using the bottom of a glass)  
1/4 cup sugar  
2-3 tbsp melted butter

mix crust ingredients and smush into the bottom of a springform pan. pour in cheesecake mixture.

bake at 450 for 10 min.  
then bake at 350 for about 45 min. (or until you can stick a fork in the middle and have it come out somewhat clean)

i like to put it in the fridge overnight so it’s cold when i sneak the first piece. it is to die for at fridge temp.
