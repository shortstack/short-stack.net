driving back from the bank…

> *on the radio…”If you see anymore traffic problems, give us a call!”*
>
> W: All of Rivers Ave is a traffic problem!
>
> B: No, it’s just that everyone in traffic on Rivers Ave has problems. And people with problems WILL have traffic problems.
>
> W: Even better when no one here wears helmets! *(SC law doesn’t require people on bikes/motorcycles to wear helmets)*
>
> B: They should ALL wear helmets.
>
> W: Even while driving a car? No one wears helmets while they’re driving.
>
> ***random guy wearing a helmet driving a MINIVAN passes us***
>
> B: THAT GUY DOES!!!

the timing couldn’t have been better. or worse, in my case.
