i don’t actually have a cube at work (don’t really want one, either) but i do have my own little area. the other day, one of my co-workers showed me [this website–Cubeecraft](http://cubeecraft.com). soon, i will have my own small army of cube people. :evil:

basically, you just need a printer, a pair of scissors, and pooossibly a utility knife for making tiny cuts.

[![](/content/images/2009/07/147a.png)](http://www.cubeecraft.com/cubee/jack-skellington)he made [jack skellington](http://www.cubeecraft.com/cubee/jack-skellington) for me. :) i picked it out and was going to do it myself but i got to work this morning and he’d already made it.

after realizing how complicated that one was, i decided to go for an easier one. i think [tux will be my first attempt](http://www.cubeecraft.com/cubee/tux). :grin:
