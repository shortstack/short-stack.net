sooo… spring break plans are almost finalized. as of now, stacey and i will be staying on daytona beach for the first half of break 40 yards from the beach and a mile from downtown daytona. the second half of break we will be staying with my cousin in miami partying it up at south beach… :cool: can we say AMAZING? for a few days, i thought plans were going to go down the drain and i’d have to stay at home and just buy a weeks worth of tanning bed visits and make margaritas. it looks like i shall be turning mexican in florida laying on a beach with cheap beer in hand. i’m so freaking psyched about this.

ALSO, good news… I GOT AN 86 ON MY BUSINESS LAW EXAM!! next week midterms start and since all my grades in my classes so far are Bs or better, i’d say i’m pretty damn happy about things.

adam is in the field until friday morning, and after my class i will be driving my happy ass down to see him. we’re getting someplace to stay, renting movies and ordering hot wings, and calling it a party. THIS HAS BEEN AN ENTIRELY TOO LONG 2 WEEKS!! i miss my baby.

i was so proud of myself today. i think i aced my econ. quiz, and then i took the bus home and cleaned EVERYTHING. yesterday i cleaned my kitchen and bathroom. today i dusted and vacuumed (mom, it’s a miracle… you know i never dust…) with the windows and door wide open because it was a GORGEOUS day and so warm with the beach music blaring and my melon/lemonade scented candles burning.

omg, i can’t wait ’til friday or the following weekend. i’m like twitching because i’m so excited.
