title: My Favorite Mashed Taters
---

this probably makes enough taters for 3 people but brett and i usually eat the whole thing. they’re my absolute favorite to make with steak and green beans :)

cut 1 russet potato into 8 blocks  
boil for about 20-30 minutes or until it falls apart with a fork

mash in a bowl

add 1/3 cup cream cheese (or however much you want, i could eat cream cheese with a spoon)  
2 tbsp butter  
1 tbsp milk  
salt and pepper to taste

mix and serve :)
