title: Sour Cream & Mushroom Chicken Casserole
---

cook 3-4 chicken breasts – boil until it shreds easily  
8 oz. sour cream  
1 can cream of mushroom soup (undiluted)

mix these ingredients thoroughly.

pour in casserole dish and cover the top with COOKED stuffing. i use [stove top chicken stuffing](http://www.kraftbrands.com/stovetop/nutrition_info.aspx?Site=1&Product=4300028521). i use the whole package. it’s delicious.

bake at 350 for about 20 minutes or until lightly browned.
