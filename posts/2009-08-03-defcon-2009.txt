defcon was freaking amazing. i made a lot of [new friends](http://alcoholfueledmachine.com), was wined and dined by goons and geeks, even got handcuffed (and learned how to get out of them). my eyes seriously welled up when i hugged everyone goodbye and got in my cab to the airport. :cry: one of my most memorable trips ever, for sure.

also, i bought entirely too much defcon swag. damn you, vendor booths and your booze.
