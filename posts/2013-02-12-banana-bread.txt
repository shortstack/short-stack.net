title: Banana Bread

![](/content/images/2013/02/20130223_1423191.jpg)

this has been one of my favorites my entire life. my mom made it all the time, and it is awesome. i like to add walnuts or mini chocolate chips to make it even more delicious.

1 1/2 cup flour  
1 tsp baking soda  
1/2 tsp salt

mix above in bowl

3 ripe bananas, mashed  
1 cup sugar  
1/2 stick butter or margarine, melted  
1 egg

mix bananas, sugar, butter and egg, add dry ingredients and bake in *greased* loaf pan for 1 hour and 15 minutes at 325 degrees.

i prefer to take it out of the pan immediately and wrap it in saran wrap/plastic wrap. this gives it a more gooey crust. if you like crispier crust, leave it uncovered for a bit.
