title: Slow Cooker Chicken Chili
---

cooked and shredded chicken breasts (i usually boil 2 chicken breasts, use however much you want)

1 jar of Newman’s Own Black Bean & Corn Salsa  
1 can black beans  
2 cans northern beans  
1-2 cups chicken broth (depends how thick or soupy you want it)  
1-2 cups cheddar cheese (i put a lot in)  
a couple dollops of sour cream

throw into slow cooker for an hour or 2 and serve :)

goes REALLY well with [these](http://www.fritolay.com/our-snacks/tostitos-artisan-recipes-roasted-garlic-black-bean.html) chips :)
