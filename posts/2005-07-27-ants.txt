the whole reason this building is noisy as crap and crawling with construction workers is because they’re rebuilding “the pit” (aka a really freaking expensive cafeteria) down the hall from me. cafeterias require chairs. today is the day they’re putting all the chairs in. outside my window this morning were hundreds of huge brown boxes all stacked up and throughout the day, i’ve been watching them take them inside, take empty boxes back outside, squish them, and put them in the disposal bin. they look like itty bitty ants going back and forth carrying flattened boxes over their heads and it’s so fun to watch because there are about 20 of them and every once in a while they’ll get pissed at eachother. some of them even have mullets. :wassat:
