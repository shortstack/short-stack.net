breaking up all my trips since august into separate entries. the original was just way too long.

sooo.. DEF CON 23.. in short:

* met a ton of people i’d been wanting to meet for a while
* hung out with my con friends
* made it the whole trip without puking
* brett and i went to a nice dinner and saw absinthe–incredible. i spilled an entire flute of champagne in my purse. also, never wearing that dress again.
* rapid7 party, was packed, open bar, lots of ballsy folks jumping in pool and getting escorted out, drew giant hermit crab on digital graffiti screen
* car hacking talk, kaminsky talk, android stagefright talk, hacker jeopardy…
* got my telephreak badge, but once again didn’t get to stay for the party
* stayed at bally’s, con was in bally’s/paris this year
* actually used the spa/gym, was super nice. and free!
* food court downstairs allowed us to not spend $50 on every single meal, but… you can only live off hotdogs for so long
* new hotel was an interesting changeup, line con was organized by day 2, just so much more walking involved
* we made a new friend at the roulette table, brett and i went out with him to margaritaville the night before freakfest
* so many red bull whiskeys on this trip
* finally got to properly meet @securityweekly and crew, next time will attempt to hang out
* actually played table games this go around… it had been a few years since i gambled
* epic trip was epic
