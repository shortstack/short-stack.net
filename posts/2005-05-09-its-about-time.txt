i’ve been waiting ages for a good scary movie to be out in theatres and finally tonight i saw a good one. ryan took me to see the amityville horror and holy hell, it kicked ass. he and i were sitting there the entire time with dumbfounded looks on our faces like we didn’t know what to think. i had chills down my back the entire movie. go ahead and laugh or make stupid comments if you want if you already saw it and think it was retarded, but i’ve not seen a movie that’s scared me in ages and it was awesome. i have a feeling i’m going to have freaking weird dreams tonight, man. it’s gonna be great.

poptarts, lemonade, and i get to sleep in tomorrow. hell yes. :smile:
