happy almost halloween everyone! thursday night was incredible. queensryche blew everyone away and it was by far the best show i have *ever* been to!! glass casket and 5 other bands played at the 828 house last night and as always, glass casket kicked ass and so did the others. i saw a shitload of people i hadn’t seen in ages and it was such a fun night. nearly everyone was dressed up and having a good time. i have a ton of pictures from both nights. queensryche pictures are up already, the others will be up later on. tonight is the halloween party at thomas’s and i’ll upload everything when i go back to boone sunday. i hope everyone has a fun, yet safe, weekend and have a great halloween. :cool: gotta get my costume and then it’s party time!
